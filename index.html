Introduction

Sondheim and Seurat. Two artistic giants, two very different mediums. One used lyric and music, and one used color and light. Both had a profound impact on their respective niches in the art community, and their legacies are sure to indure for many years. One of Sondheim's greatest works is based on Seurat's most famous painting, "A Sunday Afternoon on the Island of La Grande Jatte."
